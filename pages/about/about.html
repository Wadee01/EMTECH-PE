About the App

This app was made with the use of stackblitz.com and github.com. It was made by 4 coders using the Ionic v3 framework in stackblitz.com. https://ionicframework.com/docs/v3/ components/ was also used as a reference and guide in order to make things such as the drop down list, and buttons needed for the calculator.

To use the app, you simply have to input your gender, lifestyle, height, weight, and age. After inputting the values, you can click on the calculate, to know your BMR, Basal Metabolic rate, and DCI, Daily Calorie Intake.
